Who I Am

I am Luegi Vincent Mancao, a Marketing Management student, youth development practitioner, communications professional, and community leader dedicated to creating meaningful impact through leadership, volunteerism, advocacy, and strategic communication.

My work spans youth development initiatives, community engagement programs, leadership training, public relations, content creation, project management, and stakeholder collaboration. These experiences have strengthened my ability to lead diverse teams, manage large-scale programs, and build meaningful partnerships that create sustainable outcomes.
